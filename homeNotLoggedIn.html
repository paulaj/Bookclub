Welcome to BookClub!

Have you ever had a book recommended to you by a friend but completely forgot the title once you wanted to look it up? Maybe you stumbled upon a small bookstore and wanted to know what your friend was reading so you can get her something else by that author. BookClub is a social book manager and it's here to help you with just those problems. Recommend books to friends, save books to your "going to read" list, or show others what you are reading or have read!
